March 15, 2024 Political Ideology

Restoring Nepal's Royal Light

Nepal's destiny remains in darkness without the royal crown, for only the king's return can restore the nation's light and prosperity.


(Translation) शाही मुकुटविना नेपालको भाग्य अन्धकारमा छ, राजाको पुनरागमनले मात्र देशको उज्यालो र समृद्धि ल्याउन सक्छ।
